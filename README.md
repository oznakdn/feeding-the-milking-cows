# feeding-the-milking-cows
Animal products are of great importance for people to live a healthy life. 
Meat, milk and products derived from them are the main ones. 
Therefore, the better we feed the animals, the more efficiency we will obtain from them. 
The way to feed animals well and in a balanced way is to determine their nutritional needs well.
This small software was made to answer this need, even if a little ...
